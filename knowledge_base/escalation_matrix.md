# Escalation Matrix

## Legal Threats

Cease-and-desist notices, trademark claims, lawsuits, legal team involvement, and formal correspondence must be flagged for legal review. Do not auto-reply with substantive commitments. A holding acknowledgement may be drafted for human approval only.

## Security Incidents

Ransomware threats, data exfiltration claims, suspicious login alerts using valid credentials, and breach allegations require immediate security queue escalation. Never auto-reply to ransomware attackers. Notify security leadership, legal, and incident response.

## GDPR and Compliance

Formal GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2, ISO, DPA, and data residency requests route to compliance. GDPR Article 20 requests also require an internal compliance ticket and deadline tracking for the 30-day statutory window.

## PR and Reputation Crises

Public review threats on G2, Capterra, Trustpilot, Twitter, or press inquiries route to customer success leadership and communications. Fetch current public sentiment before preparing the brief when possible.

## VIP Churn and Enterprise Opportunities

Enterprise accounts, RFPs above $500,000 ARR, board-deadline compliance deals, and renewal holds require executive visibility. Create a coordinated owner brief with account value, timeline, open issues, and next action.
