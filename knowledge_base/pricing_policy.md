# Pricing Policy

## Plans

Standard is intended for small teams and includes shared support, base analytics, and standard API limits. Pro includes advanced workflows and higher usage ceilings. Enterprise is custom-priced and includes negotiated API limits, security review support, named support ownership, SSO, audit exports, and custom data residency options.

## Non-profit Discount

Registered non-profit organizations, including verified 501(c)(3) entities, qualify for a 30% discount on the Standard plan. The discount requires verification before contract execution and applies to seat subscriptions, not usage overages or one-time professional services.

## Academic Pricing

Universities and accredited educational programs may request academic pricing for classroom usage. Academic licenses require a named faculty sponsor, a semester term, and non-commercial usage.

## Pro-rata Billing

When a customer adds seats or upgrades mid-cycle, charge only the pro-rata amount for the remaining days in the active billing period. Downgrades take effect at renewal unless approved by finance. Billing replies should reference the current plan, number of seats, and effective date.

## Enterprise Custom Pricing

Enterprise pricing is negotiated based on seat count, support model, security requirements, API usage, data residency, and contract term. RFPs above $500,000 annual value should be routed to enterprise sales and legal.
