# SLA Policy

## Uptime Commitment

Enterprise customers receive a 99.9% monthly uptime SLA unless a separate master services agreement supersedes it. Downtime is measured from confirmed customer impact or monitoring alert, whichever is earlier, until service restoration.

## Incident Response

P0 incidents require immediate acknowledgement, incident commander assignment, and executive visibility. P0 root cause analysis must be delivered within 24 hours after mitigation. The RCA must include impact window, root cause, contributing factors, corrective actions, prevention plan, owner, and dates.

## Service Credits

Credits are calculated from monthly subscription fees and the verified downtime tier. Credits are not an admission of liability. Requests involving legal review, disputed RCA quality, or renewal hold must be escalated to customer success leadership and legal.

## Renewal Risk

If an enterprise customer places renewal on hold after an incident, create a customer-success escalation, legal review, and executive sponsor briefing. Holding replies may acknowledge receipt and explain review steps, but final commitments require human approval.
