# Compliance FAQ

## HIPAA

Business Associate Agreements are available for eligible Enterprise customers after security and legal review. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Clinical or PHI use cases must complete a HIPAA readiness questionnaire before production use.

## GDPR

GDPR Data Processing Addendum requests are handled by legal and compliance. Article 20 data portability requests require identity verification and response within the statutory 30-day window unless a lawful extension applies. These requests must not be treated as ordinary support inquiries.

## SOC 2 and ISO

SOC 2 Type II reports are available under NDA to enterprise prospects and customers. ISO 27001 questionnaires, recent penetration test summaries, subprocessors, and data residency documentation are handled through the security review process.

## Data Residency

Enterprise customers can request regional data residency subject to plan, contract term, and infrastructure availability. RFPs with multi-country residency requirements should be routed to sales engineering and legal.
